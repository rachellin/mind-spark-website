Editorial Guidelines

We here at Mind Spark believe that all content published to the platform is to maintain the utmost quality to ensure the best information for the user base. To maintain said quality, the following guidelines are listed for the benefit of any writer of the platform.

Best Practices


  • Be respectful of the subject matter and audience
  • Use language that is easily digestible for the target audience
  • For highly technical concepts, make sure to explain them thoroughly (your reader does not know what is in your head/may be unfamiliar with the topic)
  • Illustrations and graphs are highly encouraged, but not necessary
  • Try to use as reputable of sources as you can

Content


Mind Spark is a platform for users to share STEM-related content. Therefore, users should refrain from submitting content surrounding the following topics:

  • Politics
  • Distortion of facts
  • False claims in any related field that may cause danger to personal or public safety
  • Inappropriate illustrations or graphs
  • Any content seeking to harass, discriminate, or degrade particular groups (race, ethnicity, or gender)

Plagiarism Policy


  • All sources cited AND reputable
    • Wikipedia is not a source
    • Use APA citations
  • Do not repost articles that another person wrote without their expressed, written permission
