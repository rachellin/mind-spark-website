We are starting an ambassador program for Mind Spark! We are looking for people who are passionate about STEM and are willing to engage with their communities, whether within the school or with people you know. Ambassadors will be able to make pivotal decisions for Mind Spark International for each of your specific geographic areas. You will also help promote Mind Spark International in your respective areas through events, programs, social media integration.

  • strong social media presence
